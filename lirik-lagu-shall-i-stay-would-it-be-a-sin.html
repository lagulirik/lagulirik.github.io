14. conditional sentence if___(be) sick,he would stay home today


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

MATERI : CONDITIONAL SENTENCE



SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN

==========================

if___(be) sick, he would stay home today.

Kita lihat kalimat kedua disitu"he would stay", maka kalimat pertanyaan di atad merupakan conditional sentence type 2.


Jadi jawabannya adalah

If (were) sick, he would stay home today.

28. if it rained tomirriw, i .... at home all the day a. will stayed b. would stay c. would stayed d. had stay e. had stayed​


Soal:

If it rained tomorrow, I .... at home all the day

a. will stayed

b. would stay ✔

c. would stayed

d. had stay

e. had stayed

Jawab:

B. Would stay

Alasannya:

Berdasarkan Rumus conditional sentence type 2

If + S + V2 (past tense) , S + would + V1 (past future tense)

= If + it + rained tomorrow, I + would + stay + at home all day

Jadi:

If it rained tomorrow, I would stay at home all the day

= Jika hujan besok, saya akan tinggal di rumah sepanjang hari
