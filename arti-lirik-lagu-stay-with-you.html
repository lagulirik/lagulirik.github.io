18. apa arti dari "stay with me"


Answer: 

The phrase above is often used in the context of relationship, especially when one party doesn't want to be left behind by the other party. She or he would beg and say 'stay with me'. The meaning of such phrase is 
'please don't leave me alone.' or in Indonesian it can be translated as 'tetaplah bersamaku.'

Hopefully that suits your expectation. 

Kelas: SMP
Mapel: English
Kategori: vocabulary and phrases
