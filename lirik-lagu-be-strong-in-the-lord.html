12. present perfect(She/go/ to the library today) (she/not/see The Lord of the Rings) (they / be in London for six months)​


Jawaban:

She has gone to the library today

She hasn't seen The Lord of the Rings

They have been in London for six months

Penjelasan:

Rumus present perfect tense adalah:

Subject + have/has + verb-3

Have dipakai kalau subjeknya adalah:

I

You

We

They

Noun yang jamak (misalnya: the parents, the students, people, two dogs, children dll)

Has dipakai jika subjeknya adalah

He

She

It

Noun tunggal (singular) atau uncountable noun (misalnya: my father, a cat, my bedroom, the government, milk, water, one horse, dll)

Semoga bermanfaat.
